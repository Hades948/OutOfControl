## THANK YOU   
Thank you for playing my jam from the GMTK 2020 game jam! Hopefully, 
you like it but if not, please feel free to send me your feedback at

## Controls   
A = Slide left   
D = Slide right   
Spacebar = Jump   
Escape = Pause
